My Planche Program



Summary

  • Monday : Planche {1 or 2}
  • Tuesday : Planche {2}
  • Wednesday : Planche {2}
  • Thursday : Rest
  • Friday : Planche {2 or 1}
  • Saturday : Planche {1}
  • Sunday : Planche {1}
  • *

  • *The rest of the program is always in the same logic : three days of training and 1 day of rest. Like this : [122 R 211 R 122 R ...]


Planche {1} ~ 1 Hour / 1 Hour 15

  • Warm-Ups : ~ 10'

  • Planche Lean : 2 x 15" - 1' - 4'

  • Full Planche (ground) : 2 x Max - 5' - 4'30"

  • Straddle Planche (classic) (ground) : 2 x Max - 4'30" - 4'

  • Hollow Body Rocks (with bench) (50% body weight on bar) (press) : 2 x Max - 4' - 3'30"

  • Straddle Planche (on parallel bar) : 2 x Max - 3'30" - 3'

  • Hollow Body Rocks (with bench) (25% body weight on bar) (press) : 2 x Max - 2'30" - 2'

  • Tuck Planche (classic) (ground) : 2 x Max - 2' - 1'30"

  • Hollow Body Rocks (with bench) (25% body weight on bar) : 1 x Max - / - 1'

  • Planche Lean : 1 x Max - / - End of the program.


Planche {2} ~ 1 Hour / 1 Hour 15

  • Warm-Ups : ~ 10'

  • Planche Lean : 2 x 15" - 1' - 4'

  • Full Planche (ground) : 2 x Max - 5' - 4'30"

  • Straddle Planche (maltese) (ground) : 2 x Max - 4'30" - 4'

  • Straddle Planche (maltese) (parallel bar) : 2 x Max - 4' - 3'30"

  • Hollow Body Rocks (with bench) (1/3% body weight on bar) (maltese) : 2 x Max - 3'30" - 3'

  • Straddle Planche (classic) (parallel bar) : 2 x Max - 3' - 2'30"

  • Hollow Body Rocks (with bench) (25% body weight on bar) (maltese) : 2 x max - 2'30" - 2'

  • Planche Lean (maltese) : 2 x Max - 1' - End of the program.
